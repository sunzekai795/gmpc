Dataset Overview

Mental health issues have emerged as a major global public health challenge, with 71% of people with mental disorders in low-income countries lacking access to timely psychological treatment. While Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) show great potential in AI-driven psychological counseling, their development is severely hindered by the scarcity of high-quality multimodal counseling data, due to privacy constraints, insufficient professional annotations, and poor emotional expressiveness of existing synthetic data.

To address these challenges, we construct the GMPC (Generated Multimodal Psychological Counseling) Dataset, a high-quality tri-modal benchmark enriched with fine-grained psychological annotations for downstream MLLM training and evaluation in mental health support. It fills the gap of high-quality multimodal datasets in the psychological counseling field, and establishes a reliable foundation for related MLLM research.

Professional Annotation System

GMPC provides 8 categories of fine-grained annotations grounded in psychotherapy theories, all validated by certified psychological counselors.

Label Categories in GMPC
Label Name Explanation Top-3 Frequent Representations
Topic The main psychological problem of client Depression, Appearance anxiety, Partner relationship
Background Dialog context containing consultation reason and client identity -
Stage The period in which psychological issues are developing -
Guide Guidelines for therapists' speech in psychotherapy methods -
Psychotherapy Psychological treatment methods adopted by the therapist SFBT, CBT, Psychoanalytic
Reasoning Reason for the therapist to use a specific treatment method -
Client's emotion Clients' emotions in every utterance Neutral, Anxiety, Happy
Therapist's strategy Behavioral strategies taken by the therapist per utterance Question, Reflection of feelings, Reassurance

Ethics Statement

  • This study adheres to academic and global privacy guidelines including GDPR. All raw textual data are sourced from public, ethically compliant counseling resources with strict anonymization of personally identifiable information.
  • Synthetic audio/video content of GMPC is generated without replicating biometrics of real individuals, and validated by 5 licensed psychological experts to avoid harmful or misleading information.
  • The GMPC dataset is restricted to academic research only, and is prohibited for commercial or clinical use. We prioritize ethical integrity to mitigate risks while supporting AI research on mental health.
